## ARTICLE 1. SUBJECT MATTER

1.1. By this Agreement, the Provider grants the Partner the right to distribute and resell the software system "Vesta Lumina System" under the Partner's brand (white label), in accordance with the terms of this Agreement.

1.2. The Partner acquires the right to:
- Brand the Software with their own logo and colors
- Distribute the Software to their clients
- Provide first-line support to clients
- Set their own pricing for clients

1.3. The Provider retains:
- All intellectual property rights in the Software
- Control over development and updates
- Access to infrastructure and second-level technical support

## ARTICLE 8. DATA ISOLATION

### 8.1. Isolation Levels

| Tier | Isolation Type | Description |
|------|----------------|-------------|
| Silver | Shared | Shared infrastructure, logical separation |
| Gold | Shared | Shared infrastructure, logical separation |
| Platinum | Dedicated DB | Dedicated Firestore, shared compute |
| Diamond | Full Isolation | Fully isolated resources |

### 8.2. Security Measures

a) **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest  
b) **Authentication:** JWT with custom claims for isolation  
c) **Firestore rules:** Strict isolation by ownerId  
d) **Audit trail:** Full audit log of all actions  
e) **Backup:** Daily backup, 30-day retention

### 8.3. GDPR Compliance

| Role | Party |
|------|-------|
| Data Controller | End client (property owner) |
| Joint Data Processor | Partner + Vesta Lumina |

## ARTICLE 11. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 11.1. Contract Duration

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| Minimum term | 24 months |
| Notice period | 90 days |
| Automatic renewal | Annual, unless written notice |

### 11.2. Grounds for Termination

**Ordinary termination:**  
Either party may terminate the Agreement with 90 days' written notice, earliest upon expiration of the minimum term.

**Extraordinary termination (without notice):**  
a) Material breach not cured within 30 days  
b) Bankruptcy or liquidation of either party  
c) Non-payment for more than 90 days  
d) Illegal use of the Software  
e) Damage to Provider's reputation

### 11.3. Consequences of Termination

a) Partner ceases use of Software and branding  
b) Partner must notify their clients 60 days in advance  
c) Provider may offer direct migration to clients  
d) Refund: proportional for unused period  
e) Confidentiality remains in effect for 5 years
